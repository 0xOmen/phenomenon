Rules of Phenomenon

For 3 to 9 players

Players register to become prophets. Once the game starts anyone that is not a prophet can buy tickets of the prophet they think will win. Anyone owning tickets of the last living prophets wins and can claim their share of the tokens in the pool.

At the start of the game, one prophet is randomly selected to be the Chose One. The Chose One has divine powers and will never fail to perform a miracle or smite another player.

No one knows who the Chose One is, not even the Chosen One. Players must deduce this through game play. **Warning - this beta version has a known cheat that can be exploited by savvy blockchain users. Use at your own risk**

When it is a prophet's turn they can do one of three actions: Perform a Miracle, Attempt to Smite, and Accuse of Blasphemy


Starting the Game
Once the appropriate number of players have signed up for the game, any player can hit the "Start Game" button.


Perform Miracle
The Chosen One will always succeed at performing a miracle. Other players have a 75% chance of success and the odds are improved based on how many followers they have (accolites + High Priests) compared to the other living prophets. However, if they fail they die. A successful miracle will free a jailed prophet.


Attempt to Smite
A successful smite eliminates the target opponent from the game. The Chosen One will always succeed at smiting an opponent. Other players have a 10% chance of success. Chances of success go up based on the relative amount of followers a prophet has (accolites + High Priests) compared to the other living prophets. A failed Smite results in the prophet going to jail.


Accuse of Blasphemy
A successful accusal has 2 outcomes. If the target opponent is free, then they are placed in jail. If the target opponent is already in jail, then they are executed and eliminated from the game. The Chosen One has no advantage with this action. Players start with a 10% chance of success and chances of success go up based on the % of followers a prophet has (accolites + High Priests) compared to the other living prophets. Failing an accusation results in the accuser going to jail. The opponent is freed from jail if they are in jail and the accusal fails.



High Priests
At the start of the game, some prophets may randomly be chosed to be High Priests. These players do not perform actions and cannot win the game on their own, however if they are a High Priest of the last remaining prophet then they win as well. Being a High Priest improves the odds of a prophet successfully smiting an opponent or accusing them of Blasphemy. If the prophet that the High Priest is supporting dies then the High Priest is also eliminated. High Priests may change who they support at any time as long as they have not been eliminated.


Tickets to Valhalla
Any wallet not registered as a prophet can become an accolite of one of the living prophets. Accolites can buy tickets of a Prophet on a bonding curve where price per ticket equals (ticketSupply^2) / 80. Buying tickets of a prophet improves that prophet's ability to perform actions. Accolites can sell their tickets on the bonding curve as well **This will incur a 5% fee taken by the protocol**. An accolite can change who they follow but first must sell all of the tickets of the prophet they currently follow. Once an accolite has sold all of their tickets they can choose a new prophet to follow. If the prophet an accolite is following dies then the accolite is eliminated and their tickets become worthless. If an accolite is following the winning prophet at the end of the game then they also win and can claim their share of the tokens at the end of the game.


Ending and Reseting Game
When only one prophet remains, the game is over. Anyone holding tickets of the last remaining prophet can claim their share of the tokens in the pot. The game must be reset before tokens are claimable. Ticket value is deteremined by the total amount of tokens deposited minus tokens withdrawn from sale of TicketsToValhalla divided by the number of tickets held by winners. **There is a 5% fee taken by the protocol on all funds** Reset the game with the desired number of players for the next round.


Anti-Griefing - Force Turn
If after 3 minutes a player has not taken their turn ANYONE may force them to perform a miracle by clicking the Force Miracle button.
